How to Play

  1. Step 1: Click on the "Start" button to begin the game.
  2. Step 2: All cards will be revealed with images.
  3. Step 3: Memorize the images until the time runs out.
  4. Step 4: After the timeout, the cards will flip, and a question will appear.
  5. Step 5: Click on the numbered card corresponding to the image shown in the question.
  6. Step 6: If your answer is correct, you win!
